WHY CHOOSE US?

QUALITY EDUCATION

Our dedicated and experienced faculty delivers excellent teaching-learning. Tutors supervise academic performance, interact with students, and give needed guidance. In order to maintain and upgrade its benchmarks, KIST supports ongoing professional development and training of its teachers.

ACHIEVE YOUR AMBITIONS

A particularly high proportion of students at all levels successfully progress on to appropriate and sustainable further or higher education or to employment.

EXPERT SUPPORT AND GUIDANCE

Our staff will help you choose the courses that are right for you and they will help you complete your studies successfully. Your choice of course may be influenced by your future study and career plans; by your abilities, qualifications and experience; by your interests and your preferred style of learning. You might also need information and advice on a range of other issues including financial advice or study support.
